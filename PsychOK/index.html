THE PSYCHOK PSYCH MEDICINE CLINIC

EXPERIENCED, CARING & PASSIONATE

Our clinicians comprise psychiatrist and therapists who are experienced in their expertise, caring in their approach, passionate in helping their patients and proactively contributing to society. They held clinical leadership positions in the civil service and restructured hospital before entering private service.

Adelphi Psych Medicine Clinic provides assessment and mental healthcare for students and adults, we specialise in psychotherapy and medico-legal related issues such as mental capacity affecting legal competency.

The clinic offers psychotherapy, an evidence-based therapeutic approach for improving mental health well-being. Psychotherapy is individualized and may be used together with medication. The types of therapy available include:



  • Play Therapy for Children
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Clinical Psychiatry
  • Clinical Hypnosis
  • Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy
DESIGNED FOR YOUR COMFORT
The clinic is designed with the comfort of our patients and clients in mind. We are aware of the costs of living and decided to keep our charges affordable. A typical consultation cost $160. First consultation and psychotherapy session may cost more depending on complexity and duration.
